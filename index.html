Animal Care & Control

Who we are

A division of the Department of Public Works, Animal Care and Control operates the Animal Shelter and provides animal control assistance for the City. Animal Care and Control's mission is to address and respond to all animal needs in the community through education, enforcement and support in order to build a community where animals are valued and treated with kindness and respect.

Please contact us at (812)349-3492 with any animal-related questions or concerns.
