Adopting from Animal Care and Control

What We Want

Our goal is to facilitate the best match for you and your new canine or feline best friend. During the adoption counseling process we will ask questions about your previous animal experience, your lifestyle, and your expectations for a new pet, in order to try to help you find the animal of your dreams.

How to Adopt

If you find a pet you're interested in and you're able to visit, simply stop by the shelter during Adoption Center hours. Our staff and volunteers will guide you through the entire process!

Adoption Process Time

Plan for at least one hour to:

  • Meet and visit with potential pets
  • Talk with an adoption counselor
  • Complete the adoption paperwork

Adoption Center Hours

Visitations are available:

  • Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday: 12 PM - 5 PM
  • Saturday & Sunday: 12 PM - 3 PM

The shelter limits the number of visitors in kennel areas. If it's busy, you may need to wait.

What to Expect During Your Visit

  • You can view all available animals.
  • You may visit and adopt animals that do not already have pending applications.
  • Applications are processed in the order they are received by the adoption staff.

Questions?

If you have questions about the adoption process or a specific animal, email: animal@bloomington.in.gov

Adoption Fees

We at Bloomington Animal Care and Control are committed to providing the public with healthy, family-friendly animals at a reasonable cost. We are also committed to finding loving, caring, lifelong homes for one hundred percent of our adoptable feline and canine companion animals.

Adoption Fees help cover:

  • The first round of age-appropriate vaccinations, including de-worming
  • Feline Leukemia/FIV testing for cats
  • Heartworm testing for dogs
  • A free office visit with most veterinarians in Monroe County
  • Microchip identification for both dogs and cats
  • Spay/Neuter surgery for all cats, dogs, and rabbits
  • A temporary cat carrier or slip leash for dogs

Foster First Adoption Program

Foster First allows you to foster an animal for a period of seven days to see if they are the right fit for your home.

How to Participate:

  • Select and meet the animal
  • Completed adoption counseling
  • Watch our five minute orientation video
  • Sign our Foster to Adopt Adoption Contract
  • Pay Adoption Fee
